About Us

A Legacy of Transforming Lives

Founded in 2003, Concordia Comprehensive School has grown into one of Kenya's most respected institutions. Our holistic approach balances 8-4-4 and CBC curriculum excellence with character formation, sports, arts, and technology — preparing students for Kenya and the world.

Academics

Comprehensive Learning Pathways

From ECD through Form 6, our programmes are designed to challenge, inspire and equip every learner.

🧒

Early Childhood (ECD)

Play-based, inquiry-driven foundation for children aged 4–6, aligned with CBC.

  • Literacy & Numeracy
  • Creative Arts
  • Social Skills
  • Environmental Activities
📚

Lower Primary (Grades 1–3)

Core competency development through CBC with hands-on learning and mother-tongue literacy.

  • English & Kiswahili
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental Science
  • Creative Arts & Sports
🔭

Upper Primary (Grades 4–9)

Building critical thinking and STEM skills aligned with CBC competency framework.

  • Sciences & Technology
  • Social Studies
  • CRE / IRE
  • Business Studies
🎓

Secondary (Forms 1–4)

KCSE preparation with emphasis on sciences, humanities and technical subjects.

  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Mathematics
  • History & Geography
  • Computer Studies

Co-Curricular Activities

Sports, arts, debate, clubs and leadership development for whole-child growth.

  • Football, Rugby, Volleyball
  • Drama & Music
  • Science Congress
  • Student Council

ICT & STEM Track
